# Entry Hall

Connections:

North Door -> Small Library
South Door -> Outside / Arrival
West Door -> Garden

The West Door is built directly into the west wall of the Entry Hall.

Opening the West Door immediately enters the Garden.

No intermediate space exists between Entry Hall and Garden.

---

# Garden

Connection: Entry Hall West Door -> Garden East Door -> Garden.

Exactly one canonical door: east-wall door back to the Entry Hall.

The Garden East Door and the Entry Hall West Door are the same doorway connection.

The Garden is directly attached to the west wall of the Entry Hall.

The Garden connects only to the Entry Hall.

The Garden has no canonical doorway, passage, corridor, shared boundary, visual connector, path, bridge, tunnel, label-line connection, or architectural connection to the Media Antechamber, the Great Hall, or any antechamber.

In maps and diagrams, the Garden and Media Antechamber must remain visibly separate unless a future Codex release explicitly defines a canonical connection.

Northwest: History Door -> History Antechamber

Personal Antechamber: memory, identity, reflection, inner life, personal history, self-understanding.

Inventor's Antechamber: invention, mechanisms, tools, experiments, prototypes, workshop thinking.
