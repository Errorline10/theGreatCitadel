# Exterior Layer Rule

The Exterior Layer supplements Canon Core, Room Index, Room Render Definitions, Map Core, Render Core, Render Continuity Rules, and Image Reference Index.

Exterior views are room-like canonical locations. They may have stations, render defaults, reference images, and compliance checklists, but they do not override interior room topology.

Exterior architecture must map to the active interior topology wherever a direct relationship exists.

All exterior renders follow the same pre-render approval and post-render audit rules as interior renders.

# Outside of the Citadel

Type: Exterior Overview
Classification: Exterior Canonical Location

Purpose: The Outside of the Citadel is the canonical exterior representation of the Great Citadel complex as a single physical structure viewed from outside.

# Default Exterior Render

Aspect ratio: 16:9.

Default viewpoint: elevated aerial view, above the Citadel, looking northwest.

Default scale: medium-sized Citadel.

Location: atop a rocky mountain peak.

Background: distant mountain ranges.

Sky: a few steampunk blimps.

# Exterior 360 Rule

If an exterior 360 panorama is generated, it must follow the 360 Panorama Compass Normalization Rule from Render Continuity Rules: horizontal center is North, right quarter is East, left quarter is West, and far edges are the South seam.

Exterior 360 renders must also follow the full render workflow: compiled prompt, pre-render checklist, approval gate, generation, post-render compliance audit, compliance percentage, findings, and recommended GitHub save path when applicable.

No visible overlay text, arrows, compass labels, UI elements, or explanatory markings may appear unless explicitly requested.

# Exterior Structural Canon

The building is aligned north-to-south.

Required structure:

- One large main dome located on the north side.
- Eight smaller domes evenly spaced and directly integrated with the main dome structure.
- Each of the eight smaller domes has four attached smaller rooms.
- One south room attached to the main dome structure.
- The south room represents the Entry Hall exterior mass.
- A bridge connects to the Entry Hall exterior mass and extends southwest.
- The bridge terminates at a landing platform southwest of the entire Citadel.
- The Garden is attached directly to the west side of the Entry Hall exterior mass.

---

# Exterior Structural Attachment Rule

All Citadel exterior structures are physically integrated into one continuous complex.

No detached buildings. No separated structures. No free-standing annexes. No visible gaps between connected structures. No connecting hallways. No skybridges between domes. No connector tunnels.

Every dome, room mass, and architectural component must directly touch the adjacent structure.

The Citadel must read as one unified building, not many buildings connected by hallways.

---

# Exterior Dome Connection Rule

The Main Dome and the eight smaller domes are not independent buildings.

They are architectural masses integrated into a single continuous structure.

Allowed: shared walls, shared foundations, shared rooflines, shared stonework, integrated buttresses.

Not allowed: freestanding domes, covered hallways, glass corridors, outdoor connectors, bridges between domes.

---

# Exterior Topology Alignment Rule

South Room = Entry Hall Exterior Mass.

Bridge connects to Entry Hall exterior mass.

Garden is directly attached west of Entry Hall exterior mass.

---

# Exterior Garden Canon

The exterior Garden must match Garden room canon.

Required features: Zen garden, raked sand, rock garden elements, stacked smooth rocks, and one small Buddha statue.

Buddha location: northwest corner of the Garden.

Garden remains west of Entry Hall.

---

# Exterior Bridge Rule

The bridge must be fully visible in exterior overview renders.

Required: near bridge attachment point visible, entire bridge span visible, far bridge terminus visible, and both ends of the bridge in view.

The bridge may not disappear into fog, terrain, clouds, or off-frame.

---

# Landing Platform Position Rule

Landing Platform = southwest of the Citadel.

The platform is not south, southeast, east, north, floating overhead, or attached to any side except through the southwest bridge terminus.

---

# Airship Landing Platform Rule

At the far southwest end of the bridge there is one small landing platform for steampunk blimp arrival and departure.

It is small scale, functional, stone and brass, not city-sized, not a large airport, and not larger than the Entry Hall exterior mass.

---

# Airship Traffic Rule

Sky traffic remains minimal: a few distant blimps only.

Not allowed: large fleets, crowded skies, military formations, or commercial traffic corridors.

---

# Exterior Render Composition Rule

When the Aerial Overview Station is rendered, the Citadel structure, bridge, and landing platform must be visible simultaneously.

The landing platform should be visually recognizable as southwest of the Citadel.

The bridge and landing platform together should occupy roughly 10–20 percent of the total image area.

---

# Exterior Stations

1. Aerial Overview Station: above the Citadel, looking northwest. This is the default exterior station.
2. Bridge Arrival Station: viewer on the southwest bridge, looking toward the Citadel.
3. Mountain Peak Station: viewer below the Citadel, looking upward.
4. Garden Exterior Station: exterior view focused on the Garden west of the Entry Hall exterior mass.
5. North Dome Station: viewer above the northern dome complex.

# Exterior Compliance Checklist

A compliant exterior render must include a rocky mountain peak, distant mountains, a few steampunk blimps, main dome north, eight directly integrated smaller domes, south Entry Hall exterior mass, Garden west of Entry Hall exterior mass, Zen garden features, Buddha in the northwest corner of the Garden, north-south building alignment, unified structure, no visible hallways, direct structural attachment, entire bridge visible, near bridge anchor visible, far bridge anchor visible, bridge extending southwest, landing platform at southwest terminus, landing platform southwest of the entire Citadel, and one small airship landing platform.

A non-compliant exterior render includes detached dome clusters, connector hallways, skybridges, hidden bridge terminus, missing landing platform, large airport-like structure, landing platform directly south/southeast/east/north/floating, multiple landing platforms, detached Garden, Garden east of Entry Hall, bridge in any direction except southwest, missing main dome, or incorrect dome count.
