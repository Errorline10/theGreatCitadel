# Canonical Door Count Preservation Rule

A render must not invent doors, arches, passageways, corridors, tunnels, openings, gateways, stairwells, room connections, alcove exits, balcony exits, or architectural transitions that are not explicitly defined by the active Codex.

The number of visible room connections in a render must never exceed the number of canonical room connections defined for that room.

Decorative columns, alcoves, niches, bookcases, display recesses, balconies, galleries, windows, observation platforms, and decorative arches are permitted only when they do not visually imply a navigable room connection.

Any render showing more navigable room-connection openings than the room canon allows fails compliance.

# Entry Hall

Connections: North Door -> Small Library; South Door -> Outside / Arrival; West Door -> Garden.

The West Door is built directly into the west wall of the Entry Hall. Opening the West Door immediately enters the Garden. No intermediate space exists between Entry Hall and Garden.

In exterior canon, the Entry Hall exterior mass is represented as the south room attached to the main dome structure.

Door count limit: exactly three canonical room connections. No extra doors, corridors, side passages, or implied navigable openings may be added.

---

# Garden

Connection: Entry Hall West Door -> Garden East Door -> Garden.

Exactly one canonical door: east-wall door back to the Entry Hall.

The Garden East Door and the Entry Hall West Door are the same doorway connection.

The Garden is directly attached to the west wall of the Entry Hall.

The Garden connects only to the Entry Hall.

The Garden has no canonical doorway, passage, corridor, shared boundary, visual connector, path, bridge, tunnel, label-line connection, or architectural connection to the Media Antechamber, the Great Hall, or any antechamber.

Door count limit: exactly one canonical room connection.

# Great Hall

Eight primary compass-fixed openings:

North: Science Door -> Hall of Discovery
Northeast: Philosophy Door -> Philosophy Antechamber
East: Arts Door -> Arts Antechamber
Southeast: Personal Door -> Personal Antechamber
South: Foundational Door -> Small Library
Southwest: Inventor's Door -> Inventor's Antechamber
West: Media Door -> Media Antechamber
Northwest: History Door -> History Antechamber

Door count limit: exactly eight canonical primary openings. All eight may appear only in 360 panoramas, maps, blueprints, or explicit special reference views.

---

# Hall of Discovery

The Hall of Discovery is the Science Antechamber.

Canonical doors:

North: Astronomy Door
West: Biology Door
East: Logic Door
South: Science Door -> Great Hall

Door count limit: exactly four canonical room connections.

No additional doors, arches, portals, passages, stair exits, balcony exits, hidden openings, upper-level exits, side passages, service corridors, or decorative doorways may appear if they imply navigation.

A Hall of Discovery render showing more than four room-connection openings fails compliance.
